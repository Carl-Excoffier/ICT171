Project Proposal

I would like to create a website presenting the rugby team I play in. The team is called Rugbière and is part of the sport desk of the school EFREI, Paris. The objective is to present everything there is to know about the team. First, a little description with photos of each member of the desk of the team. Second, a photo gallery presenting all the events that took place this year : training, matches, team building and even school forums. Then, a calendar showing all the event that are planned for the next months. This website would serve as a internet presence of the team where people will be able to contact us but also access our social medias. If I have time, I would also like to add an entire section about the rules of rugby so that new player can get familiar with them. Other ideas to add in the future would be : - a little news page about the recent events in the professional rugby world and maybe links to cool YouTube videos, - various mini games to make people discover what rugby is like - a personal account so that each player of the team could see their statistics and make feedbacks
